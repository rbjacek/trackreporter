Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Track Reporter application collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information during its usage (“App”).

1. Information Track Reporter application collects

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address for recorded details email delivery only

1.2 Device & Usage Data

  • Log and usage data (app interactions, navigation)

1.3 Location Data

Track Reporter collects location data (GPS) to provide application features, only with your explicit permission.

1.4 Media Access

Track Reporter application uses camera, gallery and file storage. Access is only granted with user permission and solely for the stated functionality.

1.5 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Track Reporter application does not use cookies and similar technologies for trends analysis and user behavior tracking.

2. How Track Reporter application Uses Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the App

3. Permissions Required

  • Camera: To capture images during route recording
  • Location: To capture current location during route recording
  • Storage: To export recorded route details
  • Move sensor: To capture steps during route recording
  • Notifications: To handle location and move sensor services used for route recording
  • Application user email address provision: To handle recorded route details email delivery to user email address

4. Third-Party Services

Track Reporter uses Google AdMob (for ads) third-party services that may collect information.

Google AdMob service provider has its own privacy policy governing its use of information.

5. Data Retention

Track Reporter application retains user data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

You can delete your recorded data at any time by uninstalling Track Reporter application from your Android device.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Track Reporter application does not share your recorded data.

All recorded data are stored on your Android device in encrypted way.

Track Reporter application user can request sending of recorded data via email to specified email address.

7. Your Privacy Rights

For European Union (GDPR)

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your data
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability

For California Residents (CCPA)

  • The right to know what personal data is collected and how it's used
  • The right to request deletion of personal data
  • The right to opt out of sale (we do not sell data)

8. Children’s Privacy

Track Reporter application is not intended for children under the age of 18. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided with personal data, contact us immediately.

9. Security Measures

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure.

10. International Transfers

All recorded data are stored at Track Reporter application user device.

Track Reporter application user can request sending of recorded data via email to specified email address.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated within the App.
